The Problem

Businesses face significant challenges in connecting online engagement with offline experiences.

1

Digital-Physical Divide

Physical stores struggle to convert digital engagement into foot traffic and vice versa.

2

No Walk-in Incentive

Users lack incentives to visit stores beyond traditional discounts.

3

Inefficient Loyalty Programs

No simple, contactless reward mechanism for offline interactions.

How It Works

A seamless experience that rewards every physical store visit.

Check In

Users simply open the app when visiting physical locations.

Scan QR

Scan the in-store QR code displayed at the merchant location.

Unlock Rewards

On-chain rewards are instantly credited to their account.

Easy Redemption

Users can redeem rewards directly through the app.
